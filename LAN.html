LAN/WAN



There are two main types of network:

  1. Local Area Network (LAN)
  2. Wide Area Network (WAN)

A LAN covers a small area such as one site or building, eg a school or a college.

A WAN covers a large geographical area. Most WANs are made from several LANs connected together.

  1. The internet is a WAN.
  2. A school network is usually a LAN.
Network Advantages Disadvantages
LAN Low cost: Only need 2 computers connected together via a network Distributed Data: Data sharing in LAN is stored locally on each computer in the networks
WAN The scope of activities is not limited Costs for equipment and technology is very expensive
